## About HealAI

Healthcare doesn't end when a patient leaves the hospital.

Many people struggle to manage their health after a consultation. Medicines are forgotten, symptoms are ignored, follow-up appointments are missed, and medical records become scattered across different places. These small gaps can often lead to bigger health problems.

**HealAI** is an AI-powered healthcare companion designed to support patients throughout their healthcare journey. It combines intelligent conversations, health monitoring, medicine reminders, and digital health records into a single platform that helps patients stay informed and organized.

HealAI is built to **assist patients and support healthcare professionals**, not replace them.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Modern healthcare systems face several challenges that affect both patients and doctors.

Patients often:

* Forget to take medicines on time.
* Ignore early symptoms.
* Lose or misplace medical records.
* Miss regular health follow-ups.
* Find it difficult to maintain a complete health history.

Doctors, on the other hand, spend valuable consultation time understanding patient history before treatment can begin.

These challenges reduce the efficiency of healthcare delivery and delay timely medical attention.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

HealAI provides a centralized AI-powered platform where users can:

* Create a secure digital health profile.
* Interact with an AI healthcare assistant.
* Describe symptoms using text or voice.
* Receive preliminary health guidance.
* Maintain a complete digital health history.
* Receive medicine reminders.
* Track recovery progress.
* Share organized health information with doctors.

By organizing healthcare information in one place, HealAI aims to improve communication between patients and healthcare professionals while encouraging better long-term health management.

## ⚙️ How HealAI Works

1. The user registers and creates a secure healthcare profile.
2. The user interacts with HealAI through text or voice.
3. The AI collects symptoms and asks relevant follow-up questions.
4. The system stores conversations and updates the user's digital health record.
5. HealAI schedules medicine reminders and regular health check-ins.
6. Patient information is summarized for healthcare professionals.
7. Doctors review the information and provide treatment updates.
8. HealAI continues monitoring recovery while maintaining a complete digital health history.

## ⚠️ Assumptions & Limitations

### Assumptions

* Users provide accurate health information.
* Internet connectivity is available.
* Healthcare professionals verify medical decisions.

### Limitations

* HealAI provides **preliminary healthcare guidance** and should not be considered a replacement for professional medical advice.
* AI recommendations depend on the information shared by the user.
* Certain features require internet connectivity and third-party services.
